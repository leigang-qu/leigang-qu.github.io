Ph.D. candidate at NUS

NExT++ Research Center
Institute of Data Science, School of Computing
National University of Singapore

Best way to reach me is via email.

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at NExT++, National University of Singapore (NUS), supversied by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua and mentored by Wenjie Wang. Before that, I obtained my Master’s degree from Shandong University in 2022, supervised by Prof. Liqiang Nie and mentored by Prof. Meng Liu.

My research interests lie in Visual Content Generation, Multimodal Information Retrieval, and Multimodal Large Language Models.
